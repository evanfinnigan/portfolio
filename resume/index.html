School Projects

Stack Attack (CS 4480: Game Development Project - UWO)

Stack Attack is a local-multiplayer platform fighting game, in which players can choose to form & break alliances on the fly. Players stack on top of each other to gain stronger abilities against the remaining opponents, and the last stack standing wins.

Some cool features of this project include:

  • Local multiplayer
  • Player vs. AI gameplay
  • Unique alliance mechanics
  • Different abilities: melee combat and two types of projectile combat
Unity C#
