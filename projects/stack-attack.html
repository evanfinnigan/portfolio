Stack Attack!






Stack Attack! is the couch co-op brawler you've always dreamed about! Make alliances and become more powerful by stacking together with other players! Betray those alliances and battle head-to-head for ultimate victory! Work together, but trust nobody.

This was a fun project put together for the Game Development Project course, CS 4480Y, at UWO.
