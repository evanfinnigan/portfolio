Ray Tracer




A project implementing a ray-traced graphics engine, capable of rendering 3D geometry in a scene with lighting, reflections, and refractions.

  • Ray intersection of 3D Geomerty: spheres, cubes, cylinders, and planes, as well as transformations of these shapes
  • Implemented reflections and refractions for different types of material
  • Implemented Phong reflection model lighting techniques:
    • Ambient Shading
    • Diffuse Reflection
    • Specular Reflection
